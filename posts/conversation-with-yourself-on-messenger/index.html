Did you know it's possible to have a conversation with yourself on Facebook Messenger? I discovered it by accident when creating a share button for my grocery price comparison site https://allematpriser.no. It's convenient to have a convo with yourself as it makes it possible to easily share text and files between your devices.

  1. Click and wait until Facebook is loaded.
  2. Select Private Message or similar if in another language.
  3. Find yourself by searching.
  4. Click send and wait.

You can now check Messenger on your computer or phone and use your private conversation however you want.
